Privacy Policy

AI Face Attendance System

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the AI Face Attendance System ("System") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws and guidelines. The System is designed for institutional attendance management and follows ethical and responsible AI practices.

2. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Personal data is collected and processed in compliance with:

  • The Information Technology Act, 2000
  • The IT Rules, 2011 relating to Sensitive Personal Data or Information (SPDI)
  • The principles of lawful, fair, and purpose-limited processing as recognized under Indian data protection frameworks

By using this System, users provide explicit consent for data processing for attendance purposes.

3. Information Collected

The System may collect the following data:

a) Personal Information

  • Name
  • Roll number / Employee ID
  • Institutional email ID

b) Sensitive Personal Data

  • Facial images or facial feature data used for identity verification

c) Attendance Data

  • Date and time of attendance
  • Attendance status (Present/Absent)

d) Technical Data

  • Device type, browser, and basic access logs (for security purposes)

4. Purpose of Data Collection

Collected data is used strictly for:

  • Identity verification for attendance
  • Preventing proxy or fraudulent attendance
  • Maintaining accurate attendance records
  • System monitoring and performance improvement

Facial data is not used for surveillance, profiling, advertising, or any secondary purpose.

5. Consent

  • Facial and personal data are collected only after obtaining user consent
  • Consent is implied through authorized use of the System within the institution
  • Users may withdraw consent by contacting the system administrator, subject to institutional policies

6. Data Storage & Security

In compliance with Indian IT Rules, reasonable security practices are implemented, including:

  • Secure databases
  • Restricted and role-based access
  • Encrypted communication where applicable

While reasonable measures are taken, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Data Retention

Personal and facial data are retained only as long as required for attendance and institutional compliance. Data may be deleted or anonymized after the purpose is fulfilled or as per institutional retention policies.

8. Data Sharing & Disclosure

User data is not sold or shared with third parties, except:

  • With authorized institutional authorities
  • When required under applicable Indian law or legal process
  • For internal audits or system maintenance by authorized personnel

9. User Rights (as per Indian Guidelines)

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal and attendance data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Raise concerns regarding misuse or unauthorized access
  • Request deletion of data, subject to institutional and legal requirements

Requests should be addressed to the system administrator.

10. Third-Party Services

The System may use third-party services (such as cloud storage or authentication) strictly for operational purposes. Such services are selected with appropriate security and compliance measures in place.

11. Children & Student Data

This System is intended for institutional use and processes student data only with proper authorization from the institution, in line with Indian regulations.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in legal requirements or system functionality. Continued use of the System indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Information

For privacy-related concerns or data requests, users may contact the institution or the development team managing the System.
